About Blue Bear

Who is Blue Bear?

Blue Bear is a sweet little plush teddy bear who came to life one night and whose heart desires adventure and excitement while meeting new friends on his journey to Evergreen Mountain, a place where all wishes come true.

Characters

Get to know the characters

—* Blue Bear *—

Blue Bear is a sweet little plush teddy bear who came to life one night and whose heart desires adventure and excitement while meeting new friends on his journey to Evergreen Mountain, a place where all wishes come true.

—* Busy Bees *—

These little hard working soliders help Blue Bear on his journey to Evergreen Mountain. Even though they are not much bigger than a size of a pea, their magical power makes up for their size.

—* Hungry Flowers *—

One might think of flowers being beautiful and smell amazing, but I'm afraid Hungry Flowers are anything, but that. They are tricky little weeds looking for troubles around every corner. Can't trust them and definitely don't want to smell them, or they will put you in a deep sleep for days.

Stories

The stories children will love

Dive into stories that fills your heart with warm fuzzy feelings. Our stories will engage young children's interest about a Blue Bear whose life is full of wonderfull adventures.

No task is too big for Blue Bear and his friends. They go through obsticles life brings in their way.

Each story contains interactive games children will have an exciting time to play along with the books.
