The Adventures of Blue Bear

About Blue Bear

Who is Blue Bear?

Dive into stories that fills your heart with fuzzy warm feelings. Our stories engage young children's interest about a Blue Bear whose life is full of wonderfull adventure.

No task is small for Blue Bear and his friends. They go through obsticles life brings in their way.

Each story contains interactive games children will have an exciting time to play along with the books.
