The Adventures of Blue Bear

Stories

Dive into stories that fills your heart with fuzzy warm feelings. Our stories engage young children's interest about a Blue Bear whose life is full of wonderfull adventure.

No deed is small for Blue Bear and his friends. They go through obsticles life brings in their way.

Each story contains interactive games children will have an exciting time to read along with the books.
